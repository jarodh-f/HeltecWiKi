## Pioneering a New Era of Long-Range, High-Speed Wireless Connectivity for IoT

![](img/01.png)

To meet the growing demand for **long-range, high-speed, and stable wireless communication** in Internet of Things (IoT) applications, we proudly introduce the **HC-02 Wi-Fi HaLow Transparent Transmission Module**.

Powered by the advanced **ESP32-S3** and **MM6108Q** chipsets, this module offers exceptional processing capability and can be directly controlled via **UART AT commands**—no SDK development required.

<!-- truncate -->

### Compact, Efficient, and Developer-Friendly

The HC-02 adopts a **compact 1.27 mm stamp-hole (SMD)** design, making it easy to integrate into a wide range of PCB layouts. Operating in the **license-free Sub-1 GHz band**, it supports **1–2 km transmission distance** and achieves a **maximum physical data rate of up to 32.5 Mbps**.

![](img/02.png)

:::tip
This breakthrough design effectively addresses the **limited range of traditional Wi-Fi** and the **slow data rates of other IoT wireless technologies**, positioning the HC-02 as the **ideal solution for long-range, high-speed data transmission**.
:::
